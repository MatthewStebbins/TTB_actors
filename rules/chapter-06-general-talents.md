# Chapter 6: General Talents

## Overview

General Talents are the tricks, knacks, and abilities that set a character apart from everyone else — unique fighting styles, avenues of research, benefits from physical qualities, or strange twists of fate.

- Fated characters gain General Talents by advancing in Basic Pursuits (any step that grants a General Talent allows a free choice of any qualifying Talent).
- A character need only **meet the requirements** of a Talent to take it. Ceasing to meet requirements afterward does not disable the Talent.
- Many Talents have requirements involving **negative Aspect values**, representing characters who have overcome a deficiency in a unique way.
- Unless specifically stated, a character **may not choose the same Talent more than once**.
- Additional General Talents appear in supplement books (Into the Steam, Under Quarantine, Into the Bayou, etc.); always check with the Fatemaster before selecting from supplements.

## Talent Descriptions

### Advanced Training
**Requirement:** Chosen Skill Rank 5

This character has pushed mastery of a certain Skill beyond what most would consider natural.

Choose a Skill that meets the requirement. When performing a duel using the chosen Skill, increase the final duel total by **+1**.

> May be taken multiple times; each time a different Skill must be chosen.

---

### Armor Training
**Requirement:** None

This character has learned to wear armor without impeding her movements.

Reduce the **penalty to Defense from wearing armor by 1** (minimum 0).

> May be taken multiple times.

---

### Back to the Wall
**Requirement:** Tenacity 2 or higher

When there's nowhere to run, this character hunkers down and prepares for the worst.

When within **1 yard of a wall or solid object** at least as tall as the character, she gains **+1 Defense**.

---

### Better Part of Valor
**Requirement:** Tenacity −1 or lower

When the going gets tough, it's probably better to go the other way.

This character's **Charge Aspect becomes "—"** (cannot take the Charge Action), but gains **+2 to Walk Aspect**.

---

### Blissful Ignorance
**Requirement:** Cunning −1 or lower

This character is unaware of frightening details around her, which occasionally works in her favor.

When this character **fails a Horror Duel**:
- **If it is her turn**: her current Action immediately fails, but she does **not** become Paralyzed.
- **If it is not her turn**: she becomes **Slow** instead of Paralyzed.

---

### Call Shot
**Requirement:** Fated

This character's aim is quite good.

Choose a Skill. When generating a **Critical Effect** with an attack using the chosen Skill, she may **discard a card to change the Critical Effect's suit** to the suit of the discarded card.

> May be taken multiple times; each time a different Skill must be chosen.

---

### Calm And Collected
**Requirement:** Speed −1 or lower

This character has learned that rushing leads to mistakes.

This character adds **+1 to the value of any Focused Condition** she receives, to a maximum of **Focused +3**.

---

### Clear Orders
**Requirement:** Leadership 3 or higher

This character gives orders that are clear and concise.

When taking the **Order Action** to give commands to subordinate characters, this character may **Cheat Fate for those characters**.

---

### Combat Reading
**Requirement:** Cunning 1 or higher

This character reads a target during combat and strikes at openings others might not see.

When making **disengaging strikes**, add **Scrutiny Skill ranks** to the final duel total.

---

### Counterspell
**Requirement:** Counter-Spelling 3 or higher

Training in Counter-Spelling has given this character an intuitive ability to resist magic.

When this character is **targeted by an enemy's Magical Action**, the enemy **loses all suits associated with their Magical Skill**.

---

### Critical Strike
**Requirement:** Close Combat or Ranged Combat Skill 3 or higher

A skilled attacker deals more damage than a common thug.

Choose a Skill that meets the requirement. All attacks with the chosen Skill gain the following Trigger:

> **R Critical Strike:** When damaging, deal **+1 damage per R** in the final duel total.

> May be taken multiple times; each time a different Skill must be chosen.

---

### Cynic
**Requirement:** Charm −1 or lower

If it sounds too good to be true, this character gets suspicious.

Gains **+** on any duel made to **resist deception** (including Deceive and Pick Pocket Challenges, as well as the Trick Action).

---

### Defensive Protocols
**Requirement:** Leadership 3 or higher

This character organizes subordinates into defensive formations with little effort.

Subordinate characters within **a6** of this character may **flip cards when attacked**, in the same manner as a Fated character.

---

### Duck and Cover
**Requirement:** Evade 3 or higher

This character knows how to avoid flying shrapnel and roaring flames.

Gains the following ability:
> **Blast Resistant +1:** Reduce all damage from **p** and **b** effects by **+1** (minimum 1).

---

### Flick of the Wrist
**Requirement:** Might −1 or lower

This character is precise where others rely on brute strength.

When making a **Melee or Pugilism attack**, may substitute **Grace for Might** when calculating Acting value.

---

### Governor's Gift
**Requirement:** Charm 1 or higher

Some people just have a way with members of a certain gender.

Choose **male or female**. Gains **+** on Bewitch and Scrutiny duels made against members of the chosen gender.

---

### Great Fate
**Requirement:** Fated

Fate smiles upon this character, allowing her to manipulate destiny more frequently.

When drawing her **Control Hand at the end of the Prologue**, draw **one additional card**.

> May be taken multiple times; effects stack.

---

### Gruesome Attack
**Requirement:** Close Combat or Ranged Combat Skill 3 or higher

Combat is a dangerous thing, especially when this character is involved.

Choose a Skill. All **Critical Effect flips** from attacks using the chosen Skill receive **+**.

> May be taken multiple times; each time a different Skill must be chosen.

---

### Gruff
**Requirement:** Charm −1 or lower

This character speaks plainly, making it difficult for others to discern her true intentions.

Gains **+** to **Deceive and Intimidate Challenges**.

---

### Hobbling Attack
**Requirement:** Close Combat or Ranged Combat Skill 3 or higher

This character places attacks in just the right spot to slow her target down.

Choose a Skill. All attacks with the chosen Skill gain the following Trigger:

> **C Hobble:** After succeeding, the target gains the following Condition for the remainder of Dramatic Time: *"Hobbled: This character may only declare one Movement General Action per turn and may not declare the Run Action."*

> May be taken multiple times; each time a different Skill must be chosen.

---

### Honest
**Requirement:** Cunning −1 or lower

Others can see this character has an honest nature; she doesn't have to try to earn trust.

Gains **+** to all **Convince and Leadership Duels**.

---

### Imposing Mass
**Requirement:** Grace −1 or lower

This character uses her physical size to great advantage in combat.

Gains **+** to **disengaging strikes** and to any **Impose Action** she takes.

---

### Let Me Show You
**Requirement:** Leadership 3 or higher

This character has a natural affinity for teaching others.

Spend **15 minutes** and **discard a Twist Card** to explain how one of her General Talents works. The other character may then **discard a Twist Card** to gain that General Talent for the rest of the day.

- The taught character must still meet all **physical prerequisites** of the Talent.
- Talents with requirements of **Invested**, **Stitched**, or **Twisted** **cannot** be taught.

> *Updated Talent — originally appeared in Into the Steam.*

---

### Open Hand Fighting
**Requirement:** Melee 3 or higher

Having a free hand can have its advantages in combat.

If wielding a **Melee weapon** with nothing in the other hand when making a **successful disengaging strike**, the character may choose to have the disengaging strike **deal damage** to the target.

---

### Paired Weapons
**Requirement:** Close Combat or Ranged Combat Skill 3 or higher

This character effectively wields two weapons at the same time.

Choose a Skill. When wielding **two weapons of the chosen type** (one in each hand), gain **+** to all attacks made with either weapon. Despite fighting with two weapons:
- Only a **single attack flip** is made.
- Only **one weapon deals damage**; the second provides the bonus to hit.
- If wielding two **Ranged Combat weapons**, expend **one round of ammunition from each**.

> May be taken multiple times; each time a different Skill must be chosen.

---

### Plain-Spoken
**Requirement:** Intellect −1 or lower

This character is good at saying difficult truths efficiently without flowery language.

Gains **+** to **Social Duels made during Dramatic Time**.

---

### Polyglot
**Requirement:** Intellect 1 or higher

This character has a natural knack for languages.

Upon taking this Talent, choose **two languages** not already spoken and learn them. When conversing with another character in their native non-English language, gain **+** to **Barter and Bewitch duels**.

---

### Quick Study
**Requirement:** Intellect 1 or higher

Some people just learn quickly from watching others.

During the **Epilogue Skill Advancement**, may instead advance in **any Skill not yet possessed** rather than the FM-presented options, provided the character **witnessed a friendly character succeed at that Skill** during the session.

---

### Rush
**Requirement:** Speed 1 or higher

This character is adept at charging headlong into combat.

Increases **Charge Aspect by +1**.

> May be taken multiple times; effects stack.

---

### Secretive Spellcasting
**Requirement:** Magical Theory

This character has learned to hide spellcasting within the trappings of the Thalarian Doctrine.

When casting a **Spell or Manifested Power**, may **discard a card** to make her Magical Theory appear to be the **Thalarian Doctrine** to detection attempts.

---

### Seize the Day
**Requirement:** Grace −1 or lower

This character overcompensates for awkwardness by ensuring the fight ends quickly.

Gains **+** to **Initiative flips**.

---

### Shot Studies
**Requirement:** Shotgun 3 or higher

Shotguns are second nature to this character.

If this character has the **Focused Condition** and fires a shotgun loaded with **slug ammunition**, multiply the **shotgun's range by ×10**.
